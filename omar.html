Affordance

As a heavy user of music streaming services and a young gen-z who doesn't have a stable earning source, it was tough to listen to music without interuptions. With a vision to make listening to music free of cost i started building on the idea for the app.
It is tough to get money out of Indians' pockets. Netflix CEO Reed Hastings recently said the company was “frustrated” that it couldn't get subscriber growth momentum going in India. I saw this as an oppurtunity to fill the gap by ideating on a concept to disrupt the existing subscription model and build an app for the people who are using the existing music streaming services and even the ones who will start using them.
